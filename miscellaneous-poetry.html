Eikasia Collection

The Eikasia Collection is comprised of haiku motivated by monolexical prompts that were based on images, with the playful idea of excluding the image word itself. In a strict sense, however, especially in the thematic aspect, they are not traditional Japanese haiku, nor are they precisely senryū, either. Nonetheless, I like adhering to the typical structure, concerning (1) metrics, for which I use syllables and not morae or onji, in part because I do not write them in Japanese (perhaps some day), as well as, and more significantly, (2) by seeking to pivot, inspired by the disjuncture that is a distinguishing feature of haiku, and which is mediated in Japanese by the 切れ字 (kireji, ‘cutting word’). Very importantly, in the spirit of this kind of poetry, I enjoy practising its density in brevity, while playing with intuition, in connection with the meditative attitude it tends to have. For pragmatism, whenever I use the term “haiku” I mean it sensu lato, mostly in reference to my own writings. For greater accuracy, I can suggest expressions like “haikuesque” or “haiku-like” instead, as I am sure others have, but “haiku” is better-known across the globe, and its common usage in the Occidental world started in the XIX century.

Mons

Elevated joy
of conifer aroma
moss on giant rocks.

Spīra

I

Old triskelion
engraved on Celtic bronzes
Hellen brings wine jugs.

II

Apparent return
while seeing progressive shifts
the helices thread.

Unda

I

Much propagation
of global fluctuations
we chat wirelessly.

II

Landed on water
we surveyed the horizon
those are not mountains.

III

A Nippon-trotter
tells of his region's coastline
apical waters.

Arcus Pluvius

Sunward-pointing cone
where the prisms analyse
the leipreachán hides.

Horīzon

I

Vast lands before him
a pilgrim and explorer
I go where I reach.

II

Plains and rolling hills
as far as his eyes can see
liminal running.

III

He follows sunsets
at visual surface frontiers
hidden bonfire search.

Sagitta

Saxon merry men
Persian rain obscures the sun
Achilles to nought.

Mālum

Rubicund surface
the serpent doesn't know this:
green is tastier.

Strāgulum

Upon warmer fields
they arrived from colder lands
maquis for Lego.

Pantomīmus

Hefty force of grace
links to nimbleness panache
equilibrium.

Pulvis

Particles detach
when is a pile a mountain?
Residues arise.

Newborn Oracle

Baby poop.
To see baby poop.
I proclaim it as a new saying: “It is like seeing baby poop”.
That is to say, it is something about which one has no right to complain.
It is also something that one naturally will see and smell, especially when it comes to the first excretions.
Thence arises an art consisting in the reading and interpretation of feces on babies' diapers, similarly as with coffee grounds at the bottom of a cup.
Specialists in such matters, then, seek the precious and irreplaceable shits from the first one or two days after parturition.
An oracle is born.
Baby poop.

Old Occupational Exposition

I get paid to talk.
I get paid to blabber. To flap lips.
Jaw-moving and lip-flapping is what I get paid for.
Jaw down, jaw up.
Lip flap, lip flap.
One language comes in, another language comes out, and vice versa.
Input-output.
I receive currency in exchange for uttering with tongue and larynx.
I acquire money by using a flow of air to produce sounds.
Input-output.
Language in, language out, and vice versa.
One code in, another code out.
I get paid to talk.
I am a talker.
I am The Interpreter.

Addendum

Jaw down, jaw up. RQS gets paid to talk.
A blabbering mechanism.
Code in, code out.
Open the mouth. Lower the mandible.
Talk.
Mandibular hinges.
Cylinder of air.
Such are RQS's tools.

The Marble Nymph: An Erotic Poem

Blossom Yamato bardess resembling divinity;
fate-founded cornucopia for hedonistic association.
There is a kind of comic dystopia and the gods must be crazy, two.

To never unwant partaking in rituals of beauty
with faraway land kindred of priestesses of veneration;
more so when the dark side does not fully cast a shadow upon us, too.

A delicious balance in the therapy of vapour,
swaying traditional healing at its finest demonstration,
accompanied by rhythmic offerings by the muses, to Baimei Shen.

A flaring event that heralds a triumph by thyrsus
based on fundaments that proclaim harmonious optimization,
expressed in thaumaturgic libations to Aphrodite, which follow then.

Psychospheric Mnemonic Impression

Moss on rocks amid the aroma of conifers. The sound of a river flowing nearby. A refreshing mountain. The air cold enough to wear a coat. The sky clear enough to bask in the morning sun. The mist arrives late afternoon, announcing a retreat to the log cabin for bread with butter and honey, coffee and chess. The nocturnal dance of the fireflies. Tropical altitude: a magnanimous combination of elevation and latitude. It never gets hot. I see my breath, except around noon. It never gets too cold, except when it's time to sleep.

Biopsycurse

Mutually dependent structures,
triggering this diverse process,
in vicious cognition cycles.

Mutually interconnected,
fluxes circulate as programmed
and reciprocally effected.

Re-returning feedback motion:
fresh psychosocial pollution!
Intermittence of damnation.

Behavioural criticism
with naïve voluntarism?
Historic primitivism.

Epistemically-based warning,
foundational understanding.
Great danger is in forgetting.

Amiability yesterday,
hostility after today.
Precaution cannot go astray.

I assume no malice, I cannot amerce.
Your neuroendocrinological curse
thus begets mine, psychologically transverse.
