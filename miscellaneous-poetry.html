Eikasia Collection

The Eikasia Collection consists of haikuesques based on images, and associated sensations, motivated by personal moments of perception. Here is included what approximates true haiku the most. The original in Spanish is shown first and the English translation next.

I

Vientos jardíneos,
jacarandas gestuales;
olas de hierba.

Gardenean winds:
gestural jacarandas,
grass waves.

II

Cielo brillante,
sombra de jacaranda;
nube violácea.

Brilliant sky,
jacaranda shade;
violet cloud.

III

Lana montesa,
aroma conífero;
musgo en gran piedra.

Mountain wool,
coniferous aroma;
moss on great stone.
