Dianoia Collection

The Dianoia Collection consists of haikuesques on various science-fictional themes (as well as about the genre), including references to specific authors, stories, or worldbuilding contexts. The original in Spanish is shown first and the English translation next.

I

Ia

Llanura extraña,
flores luminiscentes;
aroma estelar.

Strange plains,
luminiscent flowers;
stellar aroma.

Ib

Montaña extraña,
árboles trashumantes;
fruto radical.

Strange mountain,
transhumant trees;
radical fruit.

II

IIa

Cuentos de otredad:
planetas análogos,
órbitas nuevas.

Tales of otherness:
analog planets,
new orbits.

IIb

Ficción mediata,
reaproximación real;
circuitos fluyen.

Mediate fiction,
real reapproximation;
circuits flow.

III

IIIa

Orbes brillantes,
cápsulas sensoriales;
ajusto espejos.

Brilliant orbs,
sensory capsules;
I adjust mirrors.

IIIb

Escáner neural,
cápsula hidrostática;
cuerpos clónicos.

Transneural scanner,
hydrostatic capsules;
clonic bodies.

IV

Arqueólogos:
puerta estelar antigua,
umbral futuro.

Archaeologists:
ancient stargate,
future threshold.

V

Bello cúmulo,
mas sangre en nebulosas;
vuelo en libertad.

Beautiful cluster,
but blood on nebulae;
I fly freely.

VI

Cual simulacro
anarcocapitalista;
psique infomorfa.

Simulacrum-like,
anarcho-capitalist;
infomorph psyche.

VII

VIIa

Hogar de helio:
trasfondo peregrino,
soles nómadas.

Helium hearth:
pilgrim background,
nomadic suns.

VIIb

Albergue orbital:
cosmopolita sin raíz
reposa y se va.

Orbital lodgings:
rootless cosmopolite
reposes and leaves.

VIII

VIIIa

Mozo urbanita,
pensamiento buscador;
liminaridad.

Urbanite lad,
searching thought;
liminality.

VIIIb

Tal postescasez:
domo acondicionado,
bordes místicos.

Such post-scarcity:
conditioned dome,
mystic borders.

VIIIc

Urbe en páramo,
plexo computacional:
memoria antigua.

City in wasteland,
computational plexus:
ancient memory.

IX

IXa

Galaxia-estado:
de pasados clásicos
a hoy potencial.

Galaxy-state:
from classical pasts
to potential today.

IXb

Cálculo enorme,
error psicohistórico;
flujo no lineal.

Enormous calculation,
psychohistorical error;
non-linear flux.

X

Vistas alternas,
culturas yuxtapuestas;
signos de mundo.

We alternate views
to see juxtaposed cultures
world sign variance.

XI

XIa

Impulso neural,
máquina subcelular;
niebla cuántica.

Neural impulse,
subcellular machine;
quantum mist.

XIb

Vigor de axones
en potenciales de acción:
neokinesia.

Axons' vigour
in action potentials:
neokinesis.

XII

Nutrición cíborg,
ensamble molecular;
autocuchara.

Cyborg nutrition,
molecular assembly;
autospoon.

XIII

Investigación:
cnidarios mercuriales
en Yatsushiro.

Investigation:
mercurial cnidarians
in Yatsushiro.
